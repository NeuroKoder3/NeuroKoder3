### About Me
> *"Technology and anatomy share a core principle — systems that must never fail."*

I design and develop **compliance-grade, offline-first healthcare software** — tools that remain reliable even when networks fail.  
My focus is on **transplant coordination systems**, **medical triage intelligence**, **data integrity**, and **clinical informatics** that improve operations and reduce risk.

- Creator of **TransTrack**, a HIPAA-compliant pre-transplant waitlist and operations management system  
- Creator of **TriageLink**, an AI-powered offline medical triage support system  
- Advocate for **secure, transparent, cloud-independent medical software**  
- Focused on **risk intelligence**, **compliance architecture**, and **workflow optimization**
